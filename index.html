Funny things about Giraffe

  1. Giraffes have long necks that make them look like they're always sticking their noses where they don't belong.
  2. They have a reputation for being clumsy and awkward, with their long legs and gangly bodies.
  3. Giraffes can sleep standing up, and they only need about 30 minutes of sleep each day.
  4. Despite their height, giraffes have a very low voice that sounds like a deep bassoon.
  5. Baby giraffes are born with a six-foot drop, which is quite a dramatic entrance into the world.
